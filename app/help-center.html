Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new beneficiary?

Navigate to the "Beneficiaries" page and click the "+ Add New" button. Fill out the required personal and guardian information form.

How do I export data?

Go to the "Import/Export" page from the sidebar. Select your desired data type and format (CSV, Excel, PDF), then click "Generate Export".

Can I change my password?

Yes. Go to "Settings" (Profile) page. Scroll down to the "Change Password" section, enter your current password and your new password.
